Lord Elon

Hallowed be thy name

Thy Will Be Done

on Mars as it is on Earth.


Give us this day our daily Tweet

And forgive us our trolling

as we forgive those

who quibble against us.


And lead us not into frustration

but deliver us from evil.


Give us the Sun

Free us from Traffic

Save our Planet

Make us Immortal


For thine is the kingdom of Mars,

And the Power, and the Glory,

But thine home is on Earth.

For now, at least.

Amen.
